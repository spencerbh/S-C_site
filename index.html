Join us on the Central Coast

We cannot wait for you to explore our home!

Head to Scarlett’s hometown of Los Osos to explore Montaña De Oro State Park where you can enjoy a leisure bluff trail, tide pools, or a hike with incredible views.

Visit Clay’s favorite fish and chips joint, L'il Hut, in Morro Bay and take a walk down to the Rock to meet the giant squirrels and enjoy the local sea otter groups!

If fine wine and craft beer is your fancy, don’t miss out on one of Paso Robles’s two hundred wineries or SLO local breweries. If you're feeling adventurous, please check out our favorite franklin hot springs – it’s more than meets the eye!

Come By
Plane, Train or Car

For those traveling a long distance, SLO County Regional Airport (SBP) is the closest airport and is a 10-minute drive to both the Mission and La Lomita.

For those traveling a moderate distance and would like to get even closer to the magic without getting behind the wheel, Amtrak has service to San Luis Obispo.

For those traveling a short distance, drive safe!

and Stay nearby

The Central Coast has an abundance of hotels, Airbnbs, bed and breakfasts, and inns.

If not using them for lodging, we still recommend a quick coffee and cake at The Madonna Inn or a relaxing spa day at Sycamore Mineral Springs.

So you wont be far.
